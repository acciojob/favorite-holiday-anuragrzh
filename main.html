MANALI


Manali, nestled in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is a picturesque hill station famous for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and vibrant culture. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, it offers trekking, skiing, paragliding, and serene landscapes. Popular spots include Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Hidimba Temple. Ideal for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.
